Have fun while learning counting and math in these three mini-games!
  • PIRATES GAME: Save the stranded pirates in Pirate Rescue! Count enough pirates for each rescue ship until they’re all saved!
  • ROCKETS LAUNCH: Help the rockets take off for the stars in Rocket Ship! Add and subtract fuel cells until the rockets are all fueled up and ready to go!
  • BOWLING ALLEY: Try for strikes in Bowling Alley! Can you add up your score at the end?
If you want to try another game, or start the current one over, just click the down arrow in the top right corner. Click Home to choose another game, or + to try your current game again.
